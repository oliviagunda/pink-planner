# Privacy Policy for Pink Planner

Last Updated: June 2026

## Overview

Pink Planner is a Chrome extension designed to help users organize tasks, save motivational quotes, and customize their planner experience.

We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your data secure.

---

## Information We Collect

Pink Planner does **not collect, transmit, sell, share, or store any personal information on external servers**.

All data entered into the extension remains on your device.

This may include:

- To-do list items
- Custom motivational quotes
- Image URLs added by the user
- Planner preferences and settings

---

## How Data Is Stored

Pink Planner uses Chrome's local storage APIs to save your planner data.

Data is stored locally within your browser and is only accessible to the extension.

---

## Third-Party Services

Pink Planner does not use:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- User tracking technologies
- Remote databases
- External servers

If you choose to display images from external websites (such as Pinterest image URLs), your browser may make requests directly to those websites to load the images.

Pink Planner does not control or collect information from those services.

---

## Data Sharing

Pink Planner does not:

- Sell user data
- Share user data with third parties
- Transfer user data to external systems
- Use personal information for advertising purposes

---

## Data Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, you remain in control of your information.

Removing the extension may remove stored planner data unless Chrome sync settings preserve it.

---

## Children's Privacy

Pink Planner does not knowingly collect information from children or any users.

No personal information is requested or collected.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be posted within this repository and reflected by the updated date above.
